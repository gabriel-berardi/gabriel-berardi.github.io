## 🔓 Recommended FOSS

I am a huge fan and supporter of FOSS (Free and Open Source Software). Having a global communtiy of highly talented people working together to develop great software tools is simply a wonderful idea.

Here's a list of open source software projects that I have used in the past or that I am still using and supporting:

- [Bitcoin](https://bitcoin.org/en/)
- [Linux Mint](https://linuxmint.com/)
- [Libre Office](https://www.libreoffice.org/)
- [Thunderbird](https://www.thunderbird.net/en-US/)
- [VSCodium](https://vscodium.com/)
- [Cryptomator](https://cryptomator.org/)
- [New Pipe](https://newpipe.net/)
- [Aegis](https://github.com/beemdevelopment/Aegis)
- [Bitwarden](https://bitwarden.com/de-de/)
- [F-Droid](https://f-droid.org/de/)
- [LiberaPay](https://liberapay.com/)
- [Aurora Store](https://auroraoss.com/)
- [FreeFileSync](https://freefilesync.org/download.php)
- [LibreWolf](https://librewolf.net/)
- [PiHole](https://pi-hole.net/)
- [Peergos](https://peergos.org/)
- [Cryptpad](https://cryptpad.fr/index.html)
